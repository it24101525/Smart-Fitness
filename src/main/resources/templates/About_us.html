About Smart Fitness Gyms

Empowering Sri Lankans to lead healthier, stronger lives since 1995

Our Story

Founded in 1995 by fitness enthusiast Sanith Wijesinghe, Smart Fitness began as a modest 500-square-foot facility in the heart of Colombo with just a handful of equipment and a passionate vision to transform Sri Lanka's fitness landscape.

In our early years, we faced numerous challenges—limited resources, skepticism about gym culture, and a small but dedicated clientele. However, our commitment to quality, personal attention, and results quickly earned us a reputation as the go-to fitness destination in Colombo.

By 2005, we had expanded to three locations across the city. The following decade saw explosive growth as we refined our business model, invested in cutting-edge equipment, and built a team of Sri Lanka's most qualified fitness professionals. Today, with more than 20 state-of-the-art facilities nationwide and over 50,000 active members, Smart Fitness stands as Sri Lanka's undisputed leader in fitness.

Our journey continues to be driven by our founding principle: that fitness should be accessible, enjoyable, and transformative for everyone, regardless of their starting point. As we look to the future, we remain committed to innovation, expansion, and most importantly, to being a catalyst for positive change in the lives of Sri Lankans.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is Smart Fitness different from other gyms?

Smart Fitness stands out through our commitment to quality equipment, expert trainers, and personalized member experiences. We offer unmatched variety in our facilities, innovative fitness technologies, and a supportive community atmosphere that makes every workout motivating and effective.

Do you offer personal training?

Yes, we offer comprehensive personal training services with certified fitness professionals. Our trainers hold both national and international certifications and specialize in various fitness areas including weight loss, muscle building, rehabilitation, sports performance, and senior fitness.

What's included in a membership?

Standard memberships include access to all equipment, locker rooms and showers, free fitness assessments, and basic workout guidance. Premium memberships add group classes, personalized workout programs, nutrition consultations, and guest passes.
